we are Royal International Bursary

Royal International Bursary is registered as a standard educational organisation with provisional Scholarship Services. It is a non-political, non-governmental, non-religious, and profit-making organisation operating in Global. Royal International Bursary is an organisation working for multi-services Relief and development programs in close collaboration with Different countries. The main focus is International Scholarship services to support and help young education seekers with an international scholarship to facilitate many things. Additionally, our aim is multi-service with multi sectors. Royal International Bursary was established in 2018 by a group of intellectuals who have been involved in scholarship services and community development for an extended period. The primary purposes and objectives of the royal International Bursary are to cater to the needs of the scholarships and work for students around the world.
