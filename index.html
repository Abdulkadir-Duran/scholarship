we are Royal International Bursary

Royal International Bursary is registered as a standard educational organization with provisional Scholarship -services. It is a non- political, non- governmental, non-religious, and profit-making organization operating in Global. Royal International Bursary is an organization working for multi-services Relief and development programs in close collaboration with Different countries. The main focus is International Scholarship services to support and help young education seekers with an international scholarship to facilitate many things. Additionally, our aim is multi-service with multi sectors. Royal International Bursary was established in 2018 by a group of intellectuals who have been involved in scholarship services and community development for an extended period. The primary purposes and objectives ofRoyal International Bursary are to cater to the needs of the scholarships and work for the students around the world.
