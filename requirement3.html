Requiremnets for Ph.D. Programs

If the candidate intends to apply for Ph.D. programs, the required documents are:

  • Notarized master degree

  • Certificate of proficiency in English

  • Two letters of recommendation

  • Notarized master transcripts

  • Non-criminal record

  • Passport

  • Foreigner physical examination form

  • Passport photo

  • Bank statement

  • C.V.

  • Study plan

  • Publication articles
